## Description
FitTrack AI is your personal AI-powered fitness companion. Track workouts, get personalized 7-day training plans generated by AI, and monitor your progress with detailed charts.

TRACK YOUR WORKOUTS
- Log exercises with sets, reps, weight, and duration
- Auto-calculate calories burned using MET-based formulas
- Quick-pick from 80+ exercises across strength, cardio, bodyweight, and flexibility
- Repeat recent workouts with one tap

AI WORKOUT PLANS
- Get a personalized 7-day plan based on your goals and fitness level
- Plans include warm-ups, exercises, cool-downs, and nutrition tips
- Adapts recommendations to your recent workout history
- Save and review past plans anytime

MONITOR PROGRESS
- Visualize trends with weight, calorie, and workout frequency charts
- Track your daily workout streak
- Weekly stats at a glance on your dashboard
- See your BMI and body composition trends

BUILT FOR YOU
- Dark and light mode
- Daily workout reminders and streak milestone notifications
- Clean, modern interface designed for the gym
- Secure cloud sync — your data follows you everywhere
